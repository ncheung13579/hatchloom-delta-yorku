Course

Design Thinking 101

An introductory course to human-centred design, teaching students to identify real problems, empathise with users, prototype solutions, and iterate based on feedback. Students work through the five stages of design thinking applied to a real entrepreneurial challenge.

12 blocks · Estimated 24 hours of learning
Course

Innovation Essentials

A course on identifying opportunities, generating ideas, and validating assumptions. Students learn lean startup methodology, customer discovery, and rapid prototyping — building the foundation for launching a real venture on the platform.

10 blocks · Estimated 20 hours of learning
Sandbox

Create an Opportunity

A sandbox problem where students identify a real-world opportunity and develop a viable business concept around it. Students research the market, define customer needs, and present a structured opportunity assessment to their peers.

Course

Running a Business

A hands-on course covering the fundamentals of operating a real business — from registering a venture and managing daily operations to understanding cash flow, inventory, and customer service. Students learn by running simulated businesses with real consequences.

10 blocks · Estimated 20 hours of learning
Course

Marketing 101

An introduction to marketing strategy for young entrepreneurs. Students learn to identify target audiences, craft value propositions, build brand identity, and create marketing campaigns across digital and physical channels.

8 blocks · Estimated 16 hours of learning
Course

Financial Literacy

A practical course in money management and financial decision-making. Students learn budgeting, profit and loss analysis, pricing strategy, and basic accounting — all applied to their own ventures.

8 blocks · Estimated 16 hours of learning
Event

Pitch Event

A school-organised event where students present their venture to a panel of judges. Students prepare a structured pitch covering their problem, solution, market, and financial projections. Added by Ridgewood Academy as a capstone assessment.

Quiz

Business Model Quiz

A summative assessment testing students' understanding of business model components — value proposition, customer segments, revenue streams, cost structure, and channels. Added by Ridgewood Academy as a knowledge check.

8 blocks · Estimated 16 hours of learning
